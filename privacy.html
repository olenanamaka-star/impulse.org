2. Categories of Personal Data We Collect

Data you give us

We do not ask you to provide information about yourself when you register for and/or use the Service. However, sometimes you may need to provide us your email address and some other information in communication with our Support Team in order to fulfill your request.

Data we collect automatically

Data about how you found us. We collect data about your referring app or URL (that is, the app or place on the Web where you were when you tapped on our ad).

Device and Location data. We collect data from your mobile device. Examples include: language settings, IP address, time zone, type and model of a device, device settings, operating system, Internet service provider, mobile carrier, hardware ID, and Facebook ID.

Usage data. We record how you interact with our Service — your taps on certain areas of the interface, the features and content you interact with, how often you use the App, how long you are in the App, what sections you use, and your App purchases.

Advertising IDs. We collect your Apple Identifier for Advertising ("IDFA") or Google Advertising ID ("AAID") depending on the operating system of your device. You can typically reset these numbers through your device's operating system settings.

3. For What Purposes We Process Your Personal Data

We process your personal data:

To provide our Service

This includes enabling you to use the Service in a seamless manner and preventing or addressing Service errors or technical issues. We use Crashlytics, Firebase Performance Monitoring, and Firebase Crash Reporting (provided by Google) to monitor infrastructure and the App's performance.

To manage your account and provide customer support

We process your personal data to respond to your requests for technical support, Service information, or any other communication you initiate. This includes accessing your account to address technical support requests and sending you notifications or emails about the Service's performance, security, payment transactions, or policy updates.

To communicate with you regarding your use of our Service

We communicate with you via push notifications — for example, reminders to finish your daily brain training. To opt out of push notifications, change the settings on your device. We use Firebase Cloud Messaging, Firebase Notifications, and Apple Push Notification service (APNs) for this purpose.

To research and analyze your use of the Service

This helps us maintain, improve, and develop the App and new products. We use the following analytics tools for this purpose:

  • Appsflyer — to understand how users find us and analyze Service usage. Privacy Policy
  • Facebook Analytics — aggregated demographics and interaction insights. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Remote Config — A/B testing and configuration service by Google
  • Amplitude — to understand how customers use our Service. Amplitude is EU-US Privacy Shield certified. Privacy Policy
  • Firebase Analytics — analytics service by Google. Google Privacy Policy
  • Fabric Answers / Crashlytics — product analytics by Google
  • Tableau — interactive data visualization for business intelligence

To send you marketing communications

We process your personal data for our marketing campaigns. You may receive information about our products, special offers, and advertisements in our App.

To personalize our ads

We and our partners use your personal data to tailor our ads. We use Facebook Ads Manager with Facebook Custom Audience, Google Ads, and Appodeal for ad delivery and personalization.

To opt out of personalized advertising:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Advertising → Limit Ad Tracking
  • Android: Google Settings → Ads → Opt out of interest-based ads

Additional opt-out resources: Network Advertising Initiative · Digital Advertising Alliance · DAA EU

To enforce our Terms and to prevent fraud

We use personal data to enforce our agreements, detect, prevent, and combat fraud. As a result of such processing, we may share your information with others, including law enforcement agencies.

To comply with legal obligations

We may process, use, or share your data when the law requires it.
